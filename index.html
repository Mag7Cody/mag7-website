Our Story

Mag 7 Machining was founded with a simple goal: to provide uncompromising quality and precision to industrial sectors that demand excellence.

Located in Lynnville, TN, we have grown from a small shop into a high-tech facility. Our commitment to using world-class brands like Okuma and Mitsubishi ensures that our clients receive parts that meet the tightest tolerances, every single time.

We are more than just a vendor; we are your manufacturing partner. Our team brings over 25 years of hands-on experience in complex milling, turning, and EDM work.
