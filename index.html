Our Story

Mag 7 Machining was founded with a singular goal: to provide uncompromising quality and precision to industrial sectors that demand absolute excellence.

Located in Lynnville, TN, we have evolved into a high-tech manufacturing facility. Our commitment to world-class technology ensures that our clients receive parts that meet the tightest tolerances.

Our team brings over 30+ years of experience in complex milling, turning, EDM, and high production runs.
