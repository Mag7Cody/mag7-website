Engineering Excellence

Our Capabilities

CNC Milling

3, 4, and 5-axis high-speed machining centers for complex parts in aluminum, titanium, stainless steels, and tool steels.

CNC Turning

High-precision lathe operations for cylindrical components, featuring multi-axis turning and extreme concentricity control.

Swiss Machining

High-volume, ultra-precise turning for long, slender, and complex components required in medical and defense applications.

Wire EDM

Precision wire EDM for hardened tools, dies, and ultra-fine intricate details that conventional machining cannot reach.

CAD/CAM

Fully integrated with SolidWorks and state-of-the-art CAM software for a seamless, error-free model-to-machine workflow.

Manual Machining

Traditional Bridgeports and lathes for custom one-off parts, specialized repairs, and rapid modifications.

Market Focus

Industries We Serve

Medical

Specializing in the manufacturing of high-precision orthopedic implants, including titanium cervical plates, bone screws, and specialized surgical instrumentation. We understand the critical nature of surface finish and dimensional stability in medical applications.

Aerospace

Providing flight-critical components for commercial and military aircraft. Our expertise covers intricate structural airframe parts, jet engine components, and high-concentricity fasteners machined from exotic alloys like Titanium and Inconel.

Automotive

Delivering high-performance CNC solutions for the professional racing and aftermarket automotive sectors. We specialize in custom engine blocks, suspension assemblies, and lightweight powertrain components engineered for extreme durability.

Defense

ITAR-compliant manufacturing of rugged tactical hardware and electronic housings. We provide mission-critical components for communications systems, military-grade optics, and advanced defense equipment with strict security protocols.

Our Story

Mag 7 Machining was founded with a singular, unshakable goal: to provide uncompromising quality and precision to industrial sectors that demand absolute excellence.

Located in Lynnville, TN, we have evolved from a specialized shop into a high-tech manufacturing facility. Our commitment to world-class technology like Okuma and Mitsubishi ensures that our clients receive parts that meet the tightest tolerances.

We are more than just a vendor; we are your mission-critical manufacturing partner. Our team brings over 30+ years of experience in complex milling, turning, EDM, and high production runs.
